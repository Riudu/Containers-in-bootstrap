Rajasthan

Rajasthan is the largest state of India.
It is still a monarchy until today (A monarchy under India).
The 'Raja' and the rest of the royal family reside in the Chandan Mahal in Jaipur

Rabindranath Tagore

Kaviguru Rabindranath Tagore was born in Santiniketan.
He is the author of the famous book "Gitanjali".
He has also met some famous personalities like Albert Einstein,
Helen Keller, Mahatma Gandhi, Subhas Chandra Bose, Jawaharlal Nehru, etc.
